## Short Summary

The PDF 'Teorie Completa' covers:
- **Complexity Analysis:** Big-O notation, common recurrence relations.
- **Sorting Algorithms:** Bubble, Insertion, Selection, Merge, Quick, Heap, Counting Sort.
- **Divide & Conquer & Recurrences:** Strategies for solving T(n) = a T(n/b) + f(n).
- **Binary Search Trees:** Construction, traversal, predecessor/successor.
- **Huffman Coding:** Optimal prefix codes via min-heap.
- **Graph Algorithms:** DFS, BFS; MST via Prim and Kruskal.
